About this app

An honest note on what this is — and isn't — today.

This is a demo. Everything you see is made-up sample information — not real patient data. It's here so we can show how the app will feel before it's connected to anything real.
What it does
This portal is a calm place to follow your IVF journey: your appointments, the results your clinician chooses to release, your signed consents, plain-language guides, and how-to instructions for your medicines.
What it doesn't do
  • It never decides anything medical — it only shows what your clinic shares.
  • It never works out your dose or timing — that comes from your doctor.
  • It's not for emergencies, and not a replacement for talking to your team.
